How to Use

"Title of our App" provides users with a go-to-resource to search key information and articles on natural disasters happening in 37 of the world's most high-risk countries.

Specify your search below based on country and vulnerable group, and the app will return real-time data, as well as charities and organizations involved in providing emergency and disaster relief for each given location.
